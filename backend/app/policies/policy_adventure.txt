Policy Name: Adventure Pro Travel Insurance

Overview:
Ideal for travelers participating in high-risk sports and adventure activities.

Coverage Details:

1. Medical Emergency:
   - Coverage up to $200,000
   - Covers mountaineering (below 6,000m), scuba diving, skiing

2. Rescue & Evacuation:
   - Coverage up to $300,000
   - Helicopter rescue included

3. Equipment Loss:
   - Coverage up to $5,000

4. Trip Cancellation:
   - Coverage up to $10,000

5. Accidental Disability:
   - Coverage up to $75,000

Exclusions:
- Professional competitive sports
- High-altitude mountaineering above 6,000m
