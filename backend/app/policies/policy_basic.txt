Policy Name: Basic Saver Travel Insurance

Overview:
An affordable travel insurance plan designed for short international trips with essential coverage benefits.

Coverage Details:

1. Medical Emergency:
   - Coverage up to $25,000
   - Includes hospitalization, doctor consultation, emergency surgery
   - No coverage for pre-existing diseases

2. Trip Cancellation:
   - Covers up to $2,000
   - Valid only for medical emergencies or natural disasters

3. Lost Baggage:
   - Coverage up to $800
   - Maximum $200 per item

4. Passport Loss:
   - Covers documentation assistance fees up to $300

5. Personal Liability:
   - Coverage up to $10,000

Exclusions:
- Adventure sports
- Pre-existing medical conditions
- Alcohol-related incidents

Claim Procedure:
- Inform insurer within 24 hours
- Submit hospital bills and travel documents
- Claim settlement within 10 business days
