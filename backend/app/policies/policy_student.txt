Policy Name: Student Global Study Travel Insurance

Overview:
Designed for students traveling abroad for higher education.

Coverage Details:

1. Medical Coverage:
   - Up to $150,000
   - Mental health coverage included
   - Maternity coverage included after 10 months

2. Tuition Protection:
   - Up to $15,000 if course interrupted due to illness

3. Study Interruption:
   - Travel expenses for return ticket covered

4. Laptop & Study Materials:
   - Coverage up to $2,000

5. Sponsor Protection:
   - $20,000 if financial sponsor passes away

Exclusions:
- Substance abuse
- Non-emergency cosmetic procedures

Claim Procedure:
- Inform insurer within 72 hours
- Submit university and hospital documents
- Settlement within 10 business days
