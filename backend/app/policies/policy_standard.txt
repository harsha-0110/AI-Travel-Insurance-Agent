Policy Name: Standard Explorer Travel Insurance

Overview:
Balanced coverage plan suitable for families and business travelers.

Coverage Details:

1. Medical Emergency:
   - Coverage up to $75,000
   - Includes emergency dental treatment up to $1,000
   - Covers acute onset of pre-existing conditions

2. Trip Cancellation:
   - Coverage up to $5,000
   - Covers illness, job loss, airline bankruptcy

3. Trip Delay:
   - $150 per 6 hours delay
   - Maximum $1,000

4. Lost or Delayed Baggage:
   - Coverage up to $1,500

5. Personal Liability:
   - Coverage up to $50,000

6. Emergency Evacuation:
   - Coverage up to $100,000

Exclusions:
- Extreme sports
- Cosmetic treatments abroad
